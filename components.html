6 Most Beautiful Flowers

Rose

Rose-one of the oldest flowers in the world. It is also the most recognizable and “the most famous” flower of the world.

Lotus

Lotus-The Aquatic flower blooming most beautifully from the deepest and thick mud. It is a perennially blooming flower.

Daisy

Daisy-A composite plant having a flower head with well-developed ray flowers arranged in one or a few whorls.

Sunflower

Sunflower-usually tall annual perennial plants that provides us with both oil and food. The plant is eaten by animals too.

Lavender

Lavender-A bushy, strong-scented perennial plant from the Mediterranean. It is most commonly used in aromatherapy.

Hibiscus

Hibiscus- known for their large, colorful flowers. These blossoms can make a decorative addition to a home or garden.
